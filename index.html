Zirou Qiu

Ph.D. Student in CS @ UVA; CV; Google Scholar

I am a Ph.D. stduent in Computer Science at University of Virginia working with Madhav Marathe. I am also a part of the team at the Biocomplexity Institute, working with S. S. Ravi and Dan Rosenkrantz.

I study the foundations of large-scale social systems and optimization for social goods. Specifically, I focus on graph problems arising from domains such as dynamical systems, social networks, multi-agent systems, game theory, and epidemiology.
