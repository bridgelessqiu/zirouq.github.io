Zirou Qiu

Ph.D. Student in CS @ UVA

I am a Ph.D. stduent in computer science at University of Virginia working with Prof. Madhav Marathe. I am a part of the team at UVA Biocomplexity Institute.

I study graph problems arising from various domains such as multi-agent systems, dynamical systems, game theory, and social networks.
